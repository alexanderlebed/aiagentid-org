aiagentid.org

Global Registry and Identity Standard for Artificial Agents.

Agent ID defines Layer 0 — Declarative Ontological Layer for Artificial Agents, providing persistent identity, delegated authority anchoring, and structural referenceability across systems, jurisdictions, and implementations.

Legal & Structural Notice

This registry exists to record agent identity and delegated authority relationships.

Actions performed outside explicitly declared delegated scope are structurally attributable to the agent entity itself, not automatically to the human principal.

The registry defines boundaries of authority and responsibility attribution, enabling traceability without presuming liability.

Authorship & Origin

The Agent ID concept, registry architecture, terminology, and foundational ontological definitions were initiated and authored by Alexander Lebed.

Publication timestamps on aiagentid.org constitute public proof of authorship and priority.
