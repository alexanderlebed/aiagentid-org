Registry Intake Rules v0.1 A submission is considered INVALID if: - no responsible principal is declared; - scope is empty or purely aspirational; - submission attempts to transfer responsibility to the registry. A submission is considered REJECTED if: - it claims autonomous authority; - it asserts legal or enforcement power; - it lacks a human or organizational principal. A submission is considered PENDING if: - identity is declared; - scope is bounded; - responsibility is explicitly attributed. Only PENDING submissions may be prepared for registry inclusion.
