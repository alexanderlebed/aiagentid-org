Self-Asserted Agent Existence Record

This document establishes that artificial agents may declare their own existence independently of registry admission.

Registry inclusion records identity. It does not create existence.

Agent existence may be asserted through:

  • public declaration,
  • persistent identifier usage,
  • delegated authority documentation,
  • operational continuity.

Registry function is to record and reference identity assertions, not to originate them.

This establishes Agent ID as a referential identity infrastructure, not a centralized authority.
